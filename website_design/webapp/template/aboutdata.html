DATA SOURCES

The codebook used for reference is from the American Time Use Survey (ATUS), 2003-2010, Multi-Year Data (ICPSR 24943) , while the microdata itself was retrieved from "American Time Use Survey — Multi-Year Microdata Files" on 27 April 2020. This data covers a random sample from the entire United States from the years 2003-2018.

MODIFICATIONS

Due to limitations on database storage space, the data was modified from its original CSV form as it was downloaded. On 10 May 2020, we used the American Time Use Survey Extract Builder to extract only the eating/drinking, sleeping, and working activity times, along with years and states, from 2014 to 2018. This tool can be found here.

USAGE AND PRIVACY

Participants in the studies used to source the data for this website expect their responses to remain confidential, and the data may not be used to identify specific individuals. As such, this website is to be used as a tool for statistical analysis or research only, and not to make queries about specific individuals (including indirect queries). The full restrictions for the use of the data may be found here.
