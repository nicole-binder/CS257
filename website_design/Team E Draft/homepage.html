WHY DOES IT MATTER

In unprecedented times like this, when most of us are quarantined within our houses or in campus spaces, we couldn't help but wonder how in the past, we often took going out and spending time with friends and family for granted. Often the key to understanding the demographics of a population lies not in major events or historical milestones, but instead in seemingly mundane day to day activities. These activities can unlock a whole discourse on issues from privilege to social interactions between different members and sections of the population. We seek to explore the mundane and find the extraordinary in the ordinary. We believe the tiny details can hold massive power and this is the premise behind 'What is America Doing?'.
